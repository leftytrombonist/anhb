Rehearsal Location and Map

February 24, 2025

The band is off for the summer. Rehearsals resume Wednesday August 6, 2025.

Rehearses are held every Wednesday from 1:00 - 3:00 PM The rehearsal location is Redwood Church at St. John (formerly known as ST. John UMC), 550 Mt. Paran Road, Atlanta, GA 30327. Our rehearsal hall is in the building at the rear of the property. You can park next to the nearby ballfield.
